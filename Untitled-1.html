Margarita Pizza

Sauce

  • Crush 2 tomatoes and remove seeds.
  • Mix with 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Simmer in a saucepan for 15 minutes.

Dough

  • Dissolve 1 teaspoon of active dry yeast and 1 teaspoon of sugar in 1 cup warm water.
  • Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  • Mix in 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour and 1 teaspoon of salt.
  • Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth, then let it rise for 1 hour.

Baking

  • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with a pizza stone inside.
  • Roll out dough, spread sauce, add sliced mozzarella, and fresh basil leaves.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
